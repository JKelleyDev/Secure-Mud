# Emberhold rooms — the world map.
#
# Format: each room is "[room: <id>]" followed by key: value lines.
#   title    — display name shown on 'look'
#   region   — grouping tag (used by future region events / clan control)
#   exits    — "dir=room_id, dir=room_id"  (n/s/e/w/up/down)
#   mobs     — comma-separated mob ids that spawn here (optional)
#   shop     — comma-separated item ids for sale here (optional)
#   desc     — room description. May continue on indented lines.
#
# To add a new room: copy any block, give it a unique id, link it from
# another room's exits, and (if relevant) reference its mobs/shop ids.
# A room with a mob id but no matching mob entry will crash on load —
# define the mob in mobs.txt first.

[room: town_square]
title: Emberhold Town Square
region: emberhold
exits: north=market, east=gate, south=tavern, west=guildhall
desc: Cobblestones radiate from a cracked fountain. Banners of three clans
  snap in the wind. Roads lead north to the market, east to the gates,
  south to the tavern, and west to the guildhall.

[room: market]
title: The Sunken Market
region: emberhold
exits: south=town_square
shop: rusty_sword, iron_sword, leather_armor, health_potion, torch
desc: Stalls crowd a stepped plaza. A weaponsmith eyes your coin purse.

title: Hall of Clans

[room: gate]
